Recommended Manga Of The Week #1 - unOrdinary

April 8, 2017

Summary

This webtoon is about a high school boy, named John, living in a world where everyone has powers. The strong pick on the weak and the weak accept this as a way of life and try their best to live in this status quo. John is a boy who is trying to change the status quo by showing everyone that every person is of equal value even though he is born without any powers in a world where power is everything.

Day Wait's Thoughts:

  • -Story driven
  • -For all ages
  • -Weak main character who is trying to change society's perception about how everyone is useful no matter how weak they are.

More Information:

  • -unOrdinary updates every Thursday
  • -North American webtoon artist
